# Inline formatting

**bold**, *italic*, ***bold italic***, ~~strikethrough~~, `inline code`.

Combos: **bold with `code`**, *italic [link](https://example.com)*, **[bold link](https://example.com)**, `code with **stars**`.

Sub/superscript: H~2~O and E = mc^2^. Footnote reference[^1].

A hard break:
line one
line two after a hard break.

Escapes: \*not italic\* and \`not code\`. Entities: &amp; &copy; &mdash; &alpha;.

Emphasis at word_boundaries_with_underscores and a*b*c mid-word.

[^1]: The footnote definition.
